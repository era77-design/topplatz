Deleting your Telegram account is a permanent action that removes all your chats, contacts, and data from the service. The process is initiated through Telegram's deactivation page on a web browser, requiring access to the phone number linked to your account for verification.

## What You Need

- Smartphone or computer with internet access
- Telegram account phone number
- Access to the phone linked to your Telegram account

## Steps

### Step 1: Understand Account Deletion Permanence

Before proceeding, it's crucial to understand that deleting your Telegram account is an irreversible action. All your chats, media, contacts, and other data associated with the account will be permanently wiped from Telegram's servers. There is no way to recover this information once the deletion is complete.

> 💡 **Tip:** Consider backing up any important chats or media you wish to keep before starting the deletion process.

### Step 2: Access the Telegram Deactivation Page

Telegram accounts cannot be deleted directly from the mobile app. You must use a web browser on a computer or smartphone to access the official deactivation page. Navigate to my.telegram.org/deactivate in your browser's address bar. This is the only portal for immediate account deletion.

> 💡 **Tip:** Ensure you have a stable internet connection to avoid interruptions during the process.

### Step 3: Enter Your Phone Number

On the deactivation page, you will be prompted to enter the phone number associated with the Telegram account you wish to delete. Make sure to include your full international country code (e.g., +1 for the United States). Double-check the number for accuracy before proceeding to the next step.

> 💡 **Tip:** If you have multiple Telegram accounts, ensure you are entering the number for the specific account you intend to delete.

### Step 4: Receive and Enter the Confirmation Code

After entering your phone number, Telegram will send a confirmation code. This code will NOT arrive via SMS; instead, it will be sent as a message within your Telegram app, directly to the account you're attempting to delete. Open your Telegram app, locate the message from 'Telegram' with the login code, and then enter this code into the designated field on the deactivation webpage.

> 💡 **Tip:** If you don't receive the code immediately, check your Telegram chats for any messages from the official Telegram account.

### Step 5: Confirm Your Deletion Request

Once you've entered the confirmation code, you'll see a page asking if you're sure you want to delete your account. You might also be given an optional field to provide a reason for deleting. While providing a reason is not mandatory, it can help Telegram improve its service. Click the 'Delete My Account' button to proceed.

> 💡 **Tip:** Take a moment to review the information on this page and confirm your decision.

### Step 6: Final Deletion Confirmation

As a final safeguard, Telegram will present one more confirmation prompt. This is your last chance to reconsider. Read the warning carefully, which reiterates the permanent nature of the deletion. To finalize the process, you must click 'Yes, delete my account' one last time. Your Telegram account will then be permanently removed.

> 💡 **Tip:** Once confirmed, the account and all its data are gone forever. There is no undo button.


## Tips & Tricks

- Inform your Telegram contacts about your decision to delete your account to avoid confusion or missed messages.
- If you want to take a break from Telegram without permanent deletion, consider setting a self-destruct timer in your app's Privacy and Security settings for inactive accounts.
- Remember that after deletion, your phone number can be used to create a new Telegram account, but it will be an entirely fresh profile with no previous data.

## Warnings

- ⚠️ Deleting your Telegram account is irreversible; all data, chats, and contacts will be permanently lost and cannot be recovered.
- ⚠️ Any groups or channels you created will remain, but without an owner. Consider transferring ownership before deleting if desired.
- ⚠️ You cannot recover your account or access any past conversations once the deletion is complete.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is deleting my Telegram account permanent?**

Yes, once deleted, your account and all associated data, including chats, contacts, and media, are permanently removed from Telegram's servers and cannot be recovered.

**What happens to my chats and contacts after deletion?**

All your private chats, group chat history, contacts, and media will be wiped. Your contacts will still have your phone number, but your Telegram profile will disappear from their contact lists within the app.

**Can I reactivate my account after deletion?**

No, account deletion is final. You can create a new account with the same phone number, but it will be a completely fresh profile with no previous data or chat history.

**How long does it take for my account to be deleted?**

Once you complete all the steps on the deactivation page and confirm, the deletion of your account is typically immediate.

**Can I delete my account from the mobile app?**

No, Telegram requires you to use a web browser to access their deactivation page (my.telegram.org/deactivate) to initiate and complete the account deletion process. The app only allows you to set a self-destruct timer.

**What is the self-destruct feature?**

The self-destruct feature, found in your app settings under 'Privacy and Security' > 'Delete my account if away for', allows you to set a period (e.g., 1 month, 3 months, 1 year) after which your account will automatically delete if you remain inactive.

**What if I don't have access to my phone number?**

Without access to the phone number linked to your Telegram account, you cannot receive the confirmation code needed to delete your account via the deactivation page. In this scenario, you would need to wait for the self-destruct timer to activate if you've previously set one, or try to regain access to the number.
