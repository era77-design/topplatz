To delete your Instagram account, you must use a web browser, as this option is not available directly within the mobile app. Instagram provides both temporary deactivation and permanent deletion, allowing you to choose the best option for your needs.

## What You Need

- Instagram account login credentials
- Web browser (on computer or mobile device)

## Steps

### Step 1: Access Instagram via a Web Browser

The option to delete your Instagram account is not available within the mobile app. Open your preferred web browser on a computer or mobile device and navigate to instagram.com. Log in to your account using your username and password, ensuring you have the correct credentials.

### Step 2: Navigate to the Account Deletion Page

Once logged in, you need to find the specific deletion page. The direct link is instagram.com/accounts/remove/request/permanent/. Alternatively, you can go to your profile, then Settings, Help, and search for 'Delete Account' to find the relevant link.

> 💡 **Tip:** Save time by directly navigating to the permanent deletion link provided.

### Step 3: Select a Reason for Deletion

On the 'Delete Your Account' page, Instagram will ask you to select a reason for wanting to delete your account from a dropdown menu. Choose the reason that best describes why you are leaving. This step is mandatory before proceeding.

### Step 4: Re-enter Your Password

After selecting a reason, you will be prompted to re-enter your Instagram account password to confirm your identity. This is a security measure to ensure that only the account owner can initiate the deletion process. Make sure to type it carefully.

### Step 5: Confirm Permanent Account Deletion

Once your password is confirmed, you'll see a button that says 'Delete [Your Username]'. Click this button to finalize your request for permanent deletion. Instagram will then initiate a 30-day grace period during which your account is hidden.

> 💡 **Tip:** Before confirming, consider downloading a copy of your data, including photos and videos, if you wish to keep them.

### Step 6: Understand the Deactivation Alternative

If you're unsure about permanent deletion, Instagram also offers a 'Temporarily Deactivate Your Account' option. This hides your profile, photos, comments, and likes until you reactivate it by logging back in. It's a good alternative if you just need a break.

> 💡 **Tip:** You can find the temporary deactivation option in your profile settings under 'Edit Profile', then scroll down to the bottom right.


## Tips & Tricks

- Download all your Instagram data, including photos and videos, before initiating the deletion process to avoid losing them permanently.
- Understand the difference between temporary deactivation (hides your account) and permanent deletion (removes it entirely after a grace period).
- Be aware that Instagram provides a 30-day grace period during which your account is hidden but can still be recovered by logging back in.

## Warnings

- ⚠️ After the 30-day grace period, your Instagram account and all associated data, including photos, videos, comments, likes, and followers, will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I delete my Instagram account permanently?**

To permanently delete your Instagram account, you must access the deletion page via a web browser (instagram.com/accounts/remove/request/permanent/), select a reason, re-enter your password, and confirm. Your account will then enter a 30-day grace period before permanent removal.

**How do you delete Instagram?**

You delete Instagram by logging into your account through a web browser, navigating to the account deletion page, selecting a reason for leaving, and confirming your decision with your password. The app itself does not offer this direct option.

**Can I temporarily deactivate my Instagram account instead of deleting it?**

Yes, Instagram offers a temporary deactivation option. This hides your profile and content from others, but your data is retained. You can reactivate your account anytime by simply logging back in, making it a less permanent alternative to full deletion.

**What happens when I delete my Instagram account?**

When you delete your Instagram account, your profile, photos, videos, comments, likes, and followers are hidden immediately. After a 30-day grace period, all your data is permanently removed from Instagram's servers and cannot be recovered.

**How long does it take for my Instagram account to be permanently deleted?**

Instagram initiates a 30-day grace period from the date you request deletion. During this time, your account is hidden but can be recovered by logging back in. After these 30 days, your account and all its data are permanently deleted.

**Can I recover my Instagram account after deletion?**

You can recover your Instagram account if you log back in within the 30-day grace period after requesting deletion. Once this period expires, your account and all associated data are permanently removed, and recovery is no longer possible.
